Onboarding Breakdowns

Capturing a visitor's interest and turning that visitor into a user is critical for the success of a company. Today's visitors expect an easy and seamless onboarding experience. If a visitor has trouble signing up, or a new user can't easily figure out how the product works, they often won't come back. A company has a very short timeframe to demonstrate value to gain and retain users. This must be done from the landing page through to the user's first interaction after signup. This is the key to helping a good product thrive.
